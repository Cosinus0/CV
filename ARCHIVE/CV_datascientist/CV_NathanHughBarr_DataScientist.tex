\CVItem{2016 - 2019, Roskilde University}{Master of Science in Physics and Mathematics\\
\small Master Thesis: 
\begin{itemize}
\item Title: \textit{Optimising the thickness determination of homopolymers and diblock copolymers using optical spectral reflectance during solvent vapour annealing.}
\item In this master thesis, the homopolymers Polystyrene and Polyisoprene thin film wafers and the diblock-copolymer Polystyrene-b-polyisoprene thin film wafers thickness was investigated during solvent vapour annealing using toluene.
